My Major

My major is Cybersecurity. Cybersecurity is a field that deals with the protection of computer systems, networks, programs, and data from attacks and unauthorized access. This includes the development of cyber defense tools to protect critical infrastructure as well as the analysis and mitigation of cyber threats.

My major is important because it is a rapidly growing field. As technology continues to advance, the need for cybersecurity professionals will continue to grow. Cybersecurity professionals are needed in a variety of industries, including finance, healthcare, government, and more. By studying cybersecurity, I am preparing myself for a career that is in high demand and offers many opportunities for growth and advancement.

Once I compelte my degree, I will hopefully be able to get a job in this field.
